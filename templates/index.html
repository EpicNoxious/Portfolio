about me

I'm a web developer working out of New Delhi, India, and I'm constantly looking for ground-breaking solutions to issues that arise in daily life. In my work, I spend most of my time coming up with innovative solutions to development problems.

I've been a part of this industry for over an year and within this period, I've honed my creative ability, technical and collaboration skills which makes working with a team easier. I've also had the opportunity to serve as lead web developer for different projects.

For me, each project I have the privilege to work on is a new opportunity to learn new things, meet people with ideas that differ from mine, and discover things about myself I never knew were beneath the layers of all of me.

I enjoy browsing web designs, it helps me foster new innovative ideas. I listen to lots of music in Phonk and Lo-fi genre. I spend most of my free time with my family and playing games with my buddies.

I'm surprised you actually read all of that, well thanks for your patience. I'm available for full-time roles and freelance projects worldwide. As long as you don't mind watching me work from screens away :)
